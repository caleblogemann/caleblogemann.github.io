Recent Activities:
ISU SIAM Student Chapter
During the 2017-2018 and 2018-2019 academic years, I served as President of the ISU SIAM Student chapter. As President I organized annual trips of graduate students to the SIAM Central States Section Meeting and the Midwest Numerical Analysis Days. I also organized biweekly seminars given by graduate students and faculty.
