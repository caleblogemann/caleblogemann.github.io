My name is Caleb Logemann, and I am currently a fifth year Ph.D. candidate at Iowa State University working under the direction of James Rossmanith.

I am broadly interested in computational applied mathematics. Specifically I study numerical simulation of PDEs. In particular I use Discontinuous Galerkin Finite Element Methods to numerically solve fluid mechanics equations.

I received my bachelor's degree with a triple major in mathematics, computer science, and physics from Bethel University.
