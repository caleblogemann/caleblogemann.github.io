Research Training:
  • During the summer of 2019, I had an internship at Sandia National Labs under the direction of Travis Fisher. For this internship we researched an a posteriori error estimation technique using the Defect Correction method for Summation by Parts Operators.
  • During the summer of 2017, I was the graduate mentor for an undergraduate research group (REU) funded by the NSF. This group studied a Locally-Implicit Lax-Wendroff Discontinuous Galerkin method. In particular, we researched positivity-preserving schemes for conservation laws under this method.
